Our Mission

Many people are unaware that where one lives can drastically affect the course of one’s health.

There is a lack of awareness regarding human health and its dependence on ever-changing environmental factors. According to the WHO, 24% of all estimated global deaths are linked to where someone lives. Continuing our society's mass ignorance and inaction furthers the disparities between different communities regarding the resources they do or do not receive.

Envirome's mission is to raise awareness and inspire users to take action in their own communities to mitigate health issues that are impacted by environmental factors. The world is rapidly changing, and it's time no one, not just the wealthy and those who can afford to live within gated communities, is left behind.
